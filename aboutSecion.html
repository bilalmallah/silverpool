Our Story

Established in 2004, Liverpool Speech Pathology Services has successfully treated thousands of children to overcome speech and language related difficulties.

  • 20+ Years of Experience
  • Thousands of Children Supported

Our Purpose

The driving force behind their work as Speech Pathologists is the desire to provide every child with the opportunity to communicate to the best of their ability.

  • Empowering Communication
  • Better Lives, Brighter Futures

Our Commitment

Our goal is to help individuals improve their communication abilities and work closely with families to achieve their communication goals.

  • Individualised Support
  • Family-Centred Care
